DEFEND THE MOUNTAINS

VICTORY CONDITION: Destroy your enemies!

DEFEAT CONDITION: You lose all your buildings.

SITUATION: Two other Asian tribes (A1, A2) are colonizing this island. Furthermore, they have direct access to the island's mineral resources. Your settlement, (TT) located in the East must advance quickly to the West. You cannot afford to lose access to the mountains.

1. DIARY ENTRY OF TSU TANG:

am now a wealthy man, a warrior, a ruler. But in truth I am nothing more than a poor farmer. The play of the setting sun's rays on the rice fields along the banks of the Yellow river, the enchantment of Shih-Mai's song in the evening wind--that was my fortune, my freedom. 

Now I am a captive of Ch'ih-Yu, and ruler of a land I do not understand. Even draped in with all the gold of this world, I would be a fool to believe that I were rich or free.  

Call me wealthy, call me warrior or even ruler. It may please my vanity and make the task more bearable, but it will not make it easier. And I cannot merely endure, I must prevail. 

For my fortune, for my freedom, I will again hear Shih-Mai sing."

 

Solution from:

 Marcel Jeske

 

he smaller the share your opponents have of the island (mountain with mineral resources), the weaker they will be. First, make sure you cover your considerable needs for wood and then begin expanding your territory to the north and to the south. Unneeded towers in the hinterland can be torn down. Next, build residences, a barracks, a fisherman’s hut, a gold smelting works (there is a lot of gold in the southwest), a storage area as well as a coalmine and an iron mine.

Send a couple pioneers to the west to expand your territory. There they will find some weapons. If enemy soldiers begin raids, you can send all of your soldiers there to defend your territory. By having all available soldiers attack together, you can often destroy the enemy without having to produce weapons.

 

Solution from:

 jsd98

 

t is easier to play the Asians than the Egyptians, but more difficult than the Romans. The Asians have obvious advantages in combat, but equally obvious disadvantages in construction (especially with regard to wood).

Some special tips:

  • To be able to provide a temple with alcohol, you have to build a rice farm and a distillery!

  • You should build a cannon. (you need a sulfur mine, rice farm, a gunpowder maker’s hut, and a cannon hall to do this)

  • The Asians have special spells (Stone to Iron, Melt Snow, and the Stone Curse)

  • For every bottle of alcohol, the Asians get 20% manna, whereas the Romans and Egyptians only get 10%)
