DEADLY BORDERS

VICTORY CONDITION: Conquer the entire island!

DEFEAT CONDITION: You lose all your buildings.

SITUATION: By landing on one of Ramadamses' (Ra) main islands (TT), all future trade relations have been cut off. You begin your expansion in a weakened state. Conversely his forces are quite strong. Build your settlement without trespassing inside his borders until you are ready for war!

3. DIARY ENTRY OF TSU TANG:

amadamses is an intelligent man, but that will not save him. He sacrificed the pirates in order to negotiate with me, because he believes his superior administration is enough to secure victory. Perhaps, but I know I am the stronger, and his trade only strengthens me more. As soon as we can survive on our own we will strike!  Only the remoteness of the East will remain for the Egyptian, and then I care not if he builds a pyramids to reach the gods.

I will set off for the South, to the heart of this land. To the heart of power, whose path I must follow to again find the Yellow River, my freedom, my fortune....

 

Solution from:

 Marcel Jeske

 

n this mission it is important for you to get to the abundance of raw materials in the east and to prevent your opponent from doing so. Secure your supplies of building materials and expand to the east. To the west you can acquire some extra weapons by having a couple of your pioneers expand there. With these weapons and the gold you find in the east, the rest of this mission should be a piece of cake. There is not much space but you can do without pig farms since there is more than enough fish available.

 

Solution from:

 Tobias

 

his mission is relatively easy. You simply have to find the weapons on the coast in the west and have them transported to the barracks (this happens automatically if you already have barracks). You will now be able to withstand the first attacks of your opponent and at the same time you have a good basic supply of weapons for your future army.

You have to expand to the east to get to the mountain there. At first, you’ll only be able to take a small area of the mountain because your opponent has already occupied a large part of it. With the army you already have and some newly recruited soldiers, you can vanquish your opponent. Ideally, you should attack him simultaneously on two fronts.

 

Solution from:

 Alfred Teichmann

 

egin by building a medium residence (this is always a good idea). You then have a lot of settlers available, which speeds up the construction of the following buildings. Next, build up your construction industry (woodcutter’s hut, sawmill, stonecutter’s hut) and at the same time have 10 pioneers take care of expanding  first to the west (because of the weapons lying there) and then to the east (towards the mountain). As a rule, your opponent doesn’t tend to attack although his border in the north is very near. You can, therefore, build up your industry (don’t wait too long!) in peace. Get started with the construction of a barracks early on since the weapons you find will offer you the basis for a powerful army.

My tactics: in the east slowly and carefully move toward the enemy land in the northwest. Towers are only necessary on the border itself. Before attacking, you should build a healer’s quarters on both borders. In this way you only need a relatively small attacking force that can capture an enemy tower and then retreat to the healer to be restored to full health. After a short time, your opponents are powerless against a war on two fronts and can do nothing more than watch their destroyed buildings go up in smoke.
