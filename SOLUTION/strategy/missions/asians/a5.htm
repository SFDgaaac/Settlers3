A NEW ENEMY EMERGES

VICTORY CONDITION: Conquer the entire island!

DEFEAT CONDITION: You lose your starting point.

SITUATION: The pirates colonizing to the west (P) shouldn't pose that much of a threat to you. Build up your infrastructure quickly; a small punitive expedition might also be advantageous (TT). Expect resistance from the Romans (S) in the South.

5. DIARY ENTRY OF TSU TANG:

et another civil war threatens me and I can do nothing to stop it. The last remnants of the Chinese pirates, who have plagued us for so long, saw their advantage in our struggle with Ramadamses and laid waste to our islands in the Southwest. And they had no interest in settling in the land. Their only aim was destruction and their might was not of this world.

As if that weren't enough, another foe has appeared. The Romans under Septimus Marius are settling in the South and supporting the pirates against us. I must disrupt them, or I will lose my dream of freedom.

Free, free to be poor, to cultivate my land, and to listen to the songs of Shih-Mai. Free of the need to wage war.

 

Solution from:

 Marcel Jeske

 

irst, you have to get your colony going at full speed. Build some additional woodcutter’s huts and look for raw materials. Afterwards, expand a little to the west to get weapons you’ll find lying around.

In the far west your pioneers can take land containing bars of gold. You can then use this land for building farms there and in the south. Recruit more soldiers and increase their fighting strength with the production of more gold. You can then attack the pirates either by using a couple ships (6 soldiers per ship) or by crossing Septimus‘ territory.

 

Solution from:

 Tobias

 

our opponents in the south don’t have to be defeated. You also don’t have to worry about any attacks from them. The pirates in the west are not particularly strong. You only need enough ships (three should be enough) to transport your soldiers to the pirates‘ island.

The mission should be won quickly.
