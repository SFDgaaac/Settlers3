ENDURE TO THE END

VICTORY CONDITION: Conquer the entire island!

DEFEAT CONDITION: You lose all your buildings.

SITUATION: Your foes (Ra, S), after being forced to endure set-backs,  have not been idle. They have allied against you and built up powerful industries in the hinterland. This will be your greatest challenge!

LAST DIARY ENTRY OF TSU TANG:

he time has come. We face the decisive moment. If I succeed, I will become invincible, and the Egyptian and Roman colonies will falter. If they fail to surrender, they will perish.

 Then I will once more see my fields on the banks of the Yellow River, and hear the songs of Shih-Mai, my lotus blossom.

Call me then: Tsu Tang, the farmer.

Raw materials will be in the same places as in the seventh mission. Until bread and meat become available, fish will be enough (build more farms!). You can expand to the northeast or west as you see fit. The more quickly your fighting strength increases, the easier it will be to protect yourself against attacks. Building the bakeries near the grain mills speeds up food production. As soon as your soldiers are promoted to the rank of sergeant and their fighting strength reaches 70%, you can begin with the conquest of the island. Attack Septimus first so that you get access to his gold reserves and then you can deal with Ramadamses even more effectively.
