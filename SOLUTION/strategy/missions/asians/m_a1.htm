FISH & IRON

VICTORY CONDITION: Bring 16 bars of iron and 16 fish to each of the three circles of stones on the three islands in the west!

DEFEAT CONDITION: Your colony has been completely destroyed.

 

SITUATION: There are three islands in the west, each with a Circle of Stones. In order to achieve victory you'll need to bring 16 bars of iron and 16 fish to each of these holy marks. With no spare iron at your disposal, it will be prudent to avoid conflict. Your priests have learned how to transform stone into iron, but you need 48 bars of iron altogether just for the sacrifices.

 

STORY:

h'ih-Yu has become unsettled, hungry because our sacrifices have not satiated him. Ch'ih-Yu tossed and turned and the seas boiled, the winds buffeted and our ship became but a child's toy on the waves.

But all is not lost, for Ch'ih-Yu showed mercy in the end.We found ourselves stranded, but alive. None of us are familiar with this coast, but Ch'ih-Yu has blessed us with fertile soil that should yield fine crops. 

Unfortunately, our scouts have learned that we are not alone on this island. I'm sure this must be a test, and I know we will have to appease Ch'ih-Yu if we are to be strong enough to hold off the attacks of our enemies.Our spies have discovered Circles of Stones on nearby islands. Places of sacrifice. Ch'ih-Yu demands fish and iron. There is only fish here in certain places, and possibly no iron whatsoever.

Ch'ih-Yu, the divine, may he forgive the most humble of his servants, is sometimes not only divine, but also somewhat difficult. Besides, what does he want with iron? After all, he can't eat it

 

Solution from:

 Kasuma

 

t is important to build up a thriving economy. Since you don’t have any iron, you are going to need lots and lots of stone. You are also going to need plenty of fish and iron, because later on, you will have to supply three islands with those goods. Therefore, it is a good idea to give the fish spell a try in the north, and to build some fisherman’s huts there, too. Of course, you must not use the fish for food, but bread and meat instead. It is crucial to start upgrading your soldiers early, not only because of your defense, but also because of having to cast spells for getting iron. You should crush all guard towers except for the ones at the swamp (in your opponent’s direction) to be able to concentrate your defense at that point. A hospital is sure to be of help here. Of course, you may get your first iron by casting the spell ’Heavenly Gifts’.

It is also important to build storage areas for stone and fish. You will also need a tool smith’s works, a shipyard and a pier. You should also consider getting some donkeys along with a marketplace for transporting that stone to the grave.

It is advisable to not even start expanding towards the west and south, for that would only provoke your opponent. You should regard the western rivers as your final border. However, you can still finish this map without any trouble (except for your opponent’s attack after approx. one hour). If you’ve used the spell ’Heavenly Gifts’ one or two times, and have also built a barracks, you are very likely to have ten or more fully upgraded soldiers for your defense, which should be more than enough.

 

Solution from:

 David Röthlisberger

 

inally a mission with no military conflicts!

1. See to it that there are enough rice farms along the southern swamp. For this purpose, chop down the woods next to the swamp. For your distilleries to work effectively you should have one or two rice farms more than you have distilleries. Each distillery supplies exactly one temple with the right amount of rice wine.

2. You should build one or two trade ships to be able to transport 16 irons bars and 16 fish to each of the three western islands.

3. You can place the fisherman’s huts somewhere near the sea: your fishermen are going to catch some fish sooner or later anyway. Still, you can always use spells to get some fish.

4. Getting iron bars is a bit more difficult: you will have to use your priest for that task (‘Stone to Iron’).

5. Once the required amount of fish and iron bars is on each of the three islands you’ve won!
