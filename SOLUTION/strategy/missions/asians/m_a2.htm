WINE

VICTORY CONDITION: Capture the towers on the island in the north to get hold of the wine, which your enemy has stored!

DEFEAT CONDITION: Your colony has been completely destroyed.You destroyed one of the towers you had to conquer instead.SITUATION:There is no need to destroy your opponent just yet. To attain victory you must simply take possession of the towers on the island.  This will allow you to get control of the wine.

STORY:

h'ih-Yu seems to be planning a big feast. He has ordered our priests to confiscate wine reserves which our spies have located in a large Roman colony on an island in the North.

Whatever Ch'ih-Yu has in mind, I'm sure the Romans are not about to hand over the wine on a barter basis. Too bad for them. A secret advance command of priests has managed to land on their island inconspicuously.

 

Solution from:

 David Röthlisberger

 

ou have to deal with a vastly superior opponent in this mission, but, fortunately, you have enough resources. Build up a large colony and expand your territory, if at all, with pioneers only. Crush all towers except for one and gather your troops around that single tower. That’s going to keep your opponent at bay for while.

1. Start producing lots of manna around the western swamp. Keep supplying at least two weapon smith’s works with resources to be able to produce more soldiers.

2. As soon as you can afford it, you should build two castles next to your tower, and leave a full complement of soldiers in each of them.

3. Remember to produce gold ingots.

4.  Once you have soldiers at the rank of captain, you should try to make a quick end to this mission, before your blue opponent overruns you. On the northern island two priests should have survived the attacks at the beginning of the mission. Cast the ‘Rushed Reinforcements’ spell, then click on a group of soldiers at the rank of captain. Repeat this procedure as often as your manna permits. As soon as you’ve captured all of the towers on the island, you’ve won the mission. Remember to save your game before starting your final attack!

If you want to improve your siege skills and if you are keen on giving a strong opponent a good thrashing, then you should try engaging the blue opponent in battle.

 

Solution from:

 Kasuma

 

n this mission normally only one of the priests on the northern island survives the initial attacks. However, that’s no reason to panic: one priest (or maybe two) is enough, because you have the ‘Rushed Reinforcements’ spell.

You can take your time with the towers of your yellow opponent in the north. Your first objective is to build up a thriving economy, so take up a position of all-round defense! Once you have approx. 20 – 30 bowmen and approx. 15 swordsmen (fully promoted) you can send them, one by one, to the northern island (in the northwestern corner of the island). Of course, you should not have squandered your manna.
