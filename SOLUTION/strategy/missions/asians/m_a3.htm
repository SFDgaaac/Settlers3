GOLD

VICTORY CONDITION:
Destroy all the Roman gold mines and construction sites of gold mines.

DEFEAT CONDITION: Your colony has been completely destroyed.

Your enemy owns more than 120 gold bars.

SITUATION: You must destroy all gold mines belonging to the Romans before they amass 120 bars. Speed is crucial during this mission. Make sure that the Romans don't acquire gold elsewhere.

STORY:

e have appeased Ch'ih-Yu for the moment. He now has fish and wine for his feast. Now he wants gold. Could it be that he needs the appropriate tableware?

Our Roman nemesis spread thoughout the south and far west and is rumored to have a rich supply of this rare mineral there. How convenient . This could be the solution we've been looking for, but right now the Romens are the source of our problems. The more gold they produce, the more difficult it will be to defeat them. We must hurry!"

 

Solution from:

 David Röthlisberger

 

ime is your worst enemy in this mission. 120 gold ingots are quickly produced…

1. You don’t have time to build up a proper colony. After one hour playing time your situation will become critical…

2. Produce some manna (build just one tower, two, if possible), and build two or three coalmines and an iron mine. Otherwise, you won’t be able to produce soldiers quickly enough. In any case, promote your spearmen first, then your swordsmen, and finally your bowmen.

3. Build approx. four transport ships and cast some spells (‘Heavenly Gifts’). This way you should be able to get some additional weapons and some gold.

4. Send all available soldiers to your ships. Your opponent does not recruit soldiers, but he has some soldiers at the rank of captain standing around on his territory. Still, four transport ships full of soldiers should be enough for starters…

5. Land your soldiers in the south, where, hopefully, your thief is still active. Capture as many large and small towers as possible to make sure that all of your opponent’s gold becomes yours. Leave your troops in the vicinity of the towers. Presumably, your opponent can no longer recapture any of those towers, but you never know…

6. Now you can take your time, at least for a while: you are no longer in danger of losing the game any time soon. The two gold mines are presumably destroyed already. Now the gold mine in the north, close to your second thief, has to be eliminated, too. Approx. 30 soldiers should be enough to capture the large towers nearby. Most of the time, your opponent has not built any more gold mines, and you have won by now. However, if there are still gold mines left, you’ll have to find and destroy them, too.
