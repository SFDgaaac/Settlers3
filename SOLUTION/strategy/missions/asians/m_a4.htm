THE BOUNCER

VICTORY CONDITION: Destroy the Egyptian settlement.

DEFEAT CONDITION: Your colony has been completely destroyed.

SITUATION: You enjoy a certain security from the enemy where you are, but the longer you wait, the more difficult it will be for you to destroy him. For this reason, you must quickly get your manna production in full swing and melt a narrow path through the snow (narrow ones are easier to defend). You'll need to do this before the Egyptians take over all the iron deposits.

STORY:

hatever festivities Ch'ih-Yu has planned, Horus seems intent on crashing the party. In any case, we are charged with the destruction of an Egyptian settlement, which has nested itself in on the other side of the mountain range.

There is no iron on our side of the mountain and coupled with the fact that there is not yet a passage across the snow-capped mountains, we are doubly disadvantaged. We will need to enlist the services of our priests.

 

Solution from:

 David Röthlisberger

 

he Egyptians are not going to attack you at first. However, at some point they will find a way around the mountains...

1. Since there is no iron ore to be mined on your side of the mountains, you should mine plenty of coal to get your production of manna and gold going.

2. Although you have no iron ore, you can still get some tools and weapons by letting your priests cast spells (’Stone to Iron’ & ’Heavenly Gifts’).

3. Use the few weapons you’ve gained to recruit a couple of soldiers, who should be fully promoted.

4. Once again, Ch’ih-Yu has to intervene (’Melt Snow’). Caution: your opponent will strike any minute now! Be sure to give him a warm welcome...

5. Since you have iron now, recruit 250 soldiers and overrun your opponent.
