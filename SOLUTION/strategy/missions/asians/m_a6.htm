MORE RICE WINE

VICTORY CONDITION: Conquer at least a part of the swampland so that you can build a rice farm and speed up production of rice wine. You need to have 120 barrels of rice wine in your possession.

DEFEAT CONDITION: Your colony has been completely destroyed.  

SITUATION: The swampland is centrally positioned on the main island and is controlled by a strong Roman garrison. A quick attack will surely prove beneficial. You don't have to destroy your enemy; concentrate instead on the production of rice wine.

STORY:

h'ih-Yu's list of demands continue to mount, and while the price of 120 barrels of rice wine normally wouldn't be cause for concern, this situation is - once again - difficult. An established Roman garrison controls the only swamplands on the entire island, and they apparently have instructions from their god, Jupiter, to leave Ch'ih-Yu high and dry.

I don't know the Pantheon that well, but it seems to me that they should be well supplied for the party in the meantime. Who's going to drink all that stuff anyway?

 

Solution from:

 David Röthlisberger

 

he briefing tells you to produce 120 barrels of rice wine rapidly. However, this is hard to accomplish before the blue opponent attacks, because the swamp lies far to the west and is heavily guarded by large towers and castles. In addition, once you have captured a couple of those towers, the blue opponent will immediately launch an attack, which you cannot possibly survive. Therefore, you should try capturing those towers only if you have plenty of high-ranking soldiers to secure your territorial gains. For this reason, you should try the “Large Temple” trick once again; you have plenty of space on the mainland.

1. Crush all towers except for one, and do not build any more towers. Instead, claim new territory with pioneers.

2. In the middle of the island you may find enough resources to last you well into the game. Later on, you should also claim the large mountains on the mainland to get additional resources.

3. It’s a good idea to produce lots of weapons and store them in your storage areas, until your large temples enable you to promote your soldiers. Make use of the vein of gold on the island!

4. In any case, you should try to fully promote your soldiers. As soon as you’ve accomplished that, and you have built additional mines on your mainland, you can start recruiting soldiers on a large scale.

5. With a large army (approx. 300 soldiers) you should be able to capture and hold the towers near the swamp. Build some additional rice farms, distilleries, and three storage areas in the vicinity to produce and store rice wine. Once you have produced 120 barrels of rice wine, you will be the victor!
