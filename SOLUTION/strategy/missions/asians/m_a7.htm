FIFTEEN WAITERS FOR CH'IH-YU

VICTORY CONDITION: Fifteen soldiers (of highest rank and with 90% fighting strength) must be positioned in the circle of stones located in Roman territory.

The Romans have to be destroyed.

DEFEAT CONDITION: Your colony has been completely destroyed.

SITUATION: You will find an abundance of raw materials on the two islands west and east of your starting location. You will need to have the help of your priests to get to them though. Produce manna as quickly as possible and build ships so that you can colonize these islands.Promote your soldiers to the highest ranks as rapidly as possible and then attack the guard towers surrounding the Circle of Stones.After this, you must station fifteen soldiers of the highest rank and with 90% or better fighting strength in the Circle of Stones.

STORY:

ur concentrated efforts have paid off in supplying the needs of a certain divinity, and stores are almost at capacity. Ch'ih-Yu now only needs a couple spruced-up soldiers as servants. Now, we'll need to focus on military might, producing gold for promotions. For this we need iron and gold, and unfortunately all current attempts to locate them have failed. If Ch'ih-Yu holds true to form, this will not be an easy task to fulfill.

But Ch'ih-Yu will have his feast."

 

Solution from:

 David Röthlisberger

 

e careful: there are two very strong opponents waiting for you in the south. Be sure to make good use of all the resources on both the islands in the east and west.

1. Expand your territory carefully and leave only a single tower standing.

2. Remember to produce manna at an early stage in the game. You won’t find any gold on your island and hardly any sulfur. Thanks to your ‘Heavenly Gifts’, however, you should be able to build a cannon to destroy some of your Roman opponent’s towers.

3. Once you have approx. 300 soldiers with an fighting strength of approx. 90% you should make your move. Hint: You only have to capture the Roman towers around the circle of stones! Be careful, however: as soon as you have captured a couple of those towers, the Egyptians, who have remained peaceful up to this point, will immediately strike with a huge army against you.

4. Once you have conquered all of the towers around the circle of stones, just send 15 soldiers in the circle, and the last mission of this campaign awaits you.
