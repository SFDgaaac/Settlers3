ENDURE TO THE END

VICTORY CONDITION: Take possession of eight kegs of beer, eight jugs of wine and eight baskets of gems. Raze the colony in the valley in the southeast and build a large temple in the middle of the valley.

DEFEAT CONDITION: Your colony has been completely destroyed.

SITUATION: First, you must conquer the three empires: in the southwest, northeast and in the northwest. You'll find the treasures that Ch'ih-Yu demands on islands or in storage areas. Attack the people in the valley in the southeast, take possession of all the guard towers and build a large temple in the middle of the valley.

Solution from:

 David Röthlisberger

 

difficult, but also very interesting mission awaits you. Four opponents, three of whom are going to keep attacking you, if you let them, surround you. In this mission, time is of the essence. You must recruit a vast number of soldiers, so you’d better get started at once. Let’s go!

1. Make sure you don’t waste any space: you will have to build up a large economy. The mountains in the west and northeast contain more than enough resources. Start building distilleries around the swamp early and make offerings of the rice wine immediately. It is crucial to build up your largest colony ever as quickly as you possibly can. Save your game frequently to avoid having to begin from scratch.

2. Mine some more of the gold you find in the upper left mountain in the southern part of the map.

3. You’ve already recruited some soldiers at the rank of sergeant, right? You are ready to march against the Egyptians in the northeast, once you have about 50 soldiers at this rank. Those Egyptians have only weak defenses to protect their large stocks of gold and gemstones. The gold is quite handy when it comes to boosting your soldiers’ fighting strength. At this point in the game, the Egyptians do not yet have a large army, so you can inflict quite a lot of damage without enduring severe losses. If necessary, you can always send some newly recruited reinforcements. You should even be able to vanquish the Egyptians entirely, but beware: you are sure to attract some attention from your other opponents!
Crush all towers you capture immediately to free up more soldiers, and to avoid losing those towers to your opponents again. You wouldn’t want to have to capture those towers all over again, right?

4. Heal your soldiers and keep recruiting new ones, who should be at the rank of captain by now. Once you have approx. 100 captains, you can attack the Romans in the northwest. Things are a bit more difficult here: lots of high-ranking soldiers and good defenses await you. However, your promoted soldiers should be up to the task. Keep sending newly recruited troops direct to the frontlines. Capture the towers one by one. Once you have finished off the Romans, you are free to deal with the south.

5. Now the rough times are over, so you can start recruiting a huge army. The Egyptians in the south are not much of a threat, and the second Roman tribe cannot attack you anyway.

6. The last Egyptian tribe can rely on great defenses: six castles and numerous large and small towers are waiting for your highest ranked soldiers. Make sure your fighting strength is higher than 100%.

7. As soon as you’ve managed to get through the second Egyptian tribe’s defenses, the last opponent in the southeast will already be waiting for you. Make sure to fill up the ranks of your army (250 soldiers, at least) before you engage your opponent!

8. Once you have recruited enough soldiers, all you need are priests: with the help of their spell ‘Melting Snow’ you should create a passageway from the west into the Romans’ territory. Your soldiers will ‘only’ encounter small towers, so you should be able to gain a foothold in your opponent’s territory. Conquer the entire valley and build a large temple at its center. Victory is yours! Congratulations, you’ve led the Asians home!
