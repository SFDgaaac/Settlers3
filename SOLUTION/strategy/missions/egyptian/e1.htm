THE HUNT FOR GOLD

VICTORY CONDITION:
Conquer the entire island!

DEFEAT CONDITION:
You lose all your buildings.

SITUATION:
A small Egyptian colony (E) has a foothold in the Northwest. Unfortunately, this colony has the only gold resources on the island. This gives the enemy a dangerous advantage.

am a sculptor, a sculptor at Pharaoh's court. I struggled to get that post, and now Horus appears and I am Pharaoh? Solution from:

 Marcel Jeske

 

egin by building two stonecutter's huts and a woodcutter's hut and then a medium residence, a guard tower and a sawmill. Convert five of the settlers that come out of the residence into pioneers and send them to work in the west. You'll find gold near a shipwreck. The pioneers will capture the land containing the gold and the fighting strength of your small troop of soldiers will rise by about 9%.

Next, expand northward quickly and secure area in the mountains for mining mineral resources. Make sure you have access to the small deposit of gems there.

Now you can focus on your economy. Your computer opponent will soon be disturbed by your buildup and begin attacking your towers in the north. Just send the rest of your soldiers to the area and your opponent will flee. As soon as the fighting strength of your soldiers has risen to about 75% on account of the gems, build two pyramids so that you can promote your swordsmen (there is enough stone). Make sure you've already adjusted the entire production of weapons exclusively to swords. With an army of about 25 swordsmen you should have an easy time with your opponent.
