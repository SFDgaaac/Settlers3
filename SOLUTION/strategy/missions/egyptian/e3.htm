THE BIG ISLAND

VICTORY CONDITION:
Destroy settlement of Tsu Tang!

DEFEAT CONDITION:
You lose all your buildings.

SITUATION:
Your victory on the big island has earned you new territory and a new enemy(Ra). You landed on an unexplored island in Northwest. A strong Asian colony has formed in the Southeast (TT), they must be wiped out.

3. DIARY ENTRY OF RAMADAMSES:

Solution from:

 Marcel Jeske

 

ear down the forester's hut at once before he covers up valuable land for building. There are more than enough trees so you won't need a forester in this mission. In the south you'll find additional raw materials. Be careful about advancing too far eastwards because, if you do, your opponent will attack early and at this stage, you'll be greatly outmatched.

In the south, fell all the trees you need to build up your heavy industry. You can then advance to the west when you have enough free settlers. Take land with pioneers. You'll find a lot of trees, mineral resources and land for building on the other side of the swamp. Promote your troops as early as possible by accumulating gold and gems. There are gems to be found but it is not easy.

They can be found where the opponent's mountains form a "U" rotated toward the west. They are in the upper part of the mountains.

As soon as you have manna, you can have a priest drain the swamp and then you can construct more buildings to promote your soldiers. Your computer opponent promotes his soldiers quite early. If you've withstood all his attacks and have gathered enough soldiers together, you can begin with a long campaign of conquest. Attack your enemy first in the south where he has his temples and barracks.

 

Solution from:

 Steffen Köhler

 

f you are constantly attacked by enemy forces before your colony is complete and before your production is in full swing, you can try the following strategy. After failing for the third time, I saw no other effective remedy. Gather all of your soldiers together – you should have about 15 swordsmen. Move them to the east behind the mountain and attack. At the beginning of the game, there are three guard towers there. Your opponent is strong, but with a little luck you'll manage. My best time for getting through this mission is four minutes and 51 seconds.

 

Solution from:

 Clemens Adolphs

 

ince there is little space at the beginning, you should move to the south as soon as possible and then continue expanding to the east so that the Asians don't get control of the entire mountain area. Even when you do this, space is at a premium. Concentrate on increasing your troops as quickly as possible; promoting your soldiers is not absolutely necessary.

As soon as you have 50-60 soldiers and also enough gems (at least 60% fighting strength), you can send a thief into enemy territory to find out where enemy soldiers are concentrated. Now look for a tower that is in such a location that the enemy soldiers have to pass through the mountains in single file (they cannot cross snow) to defend it. You can then weaken the enemy significantly if you have enough bowmen (about 15-20). Then, simply proceed from tower to tower to defeat your opponent.
