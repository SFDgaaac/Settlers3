INTO THE WEST

VICTORY CONDITION:
Defeat Tsu Tang!

DEFEAT CONDITION:
You lose your western outpost (Ra2).

SITUATION:
You'll (Ra1, Ra2) have to advance quickly to the west in order to avoid insurmountable resistance. Despite your still fragile infrastructure, you should not wait until Tsu Tang (TT) has secured his position.

You'll have to hold and strengthen your outpost, even though it will be difficult supplying it in the beginning. At the same time, while attacking Tsu Tang, you should to be prepared to face resistance from the Romans in the South (S).The Romans will not continue the fight once Tsu Tang has been defeated, they will pursue other goals.

et your building industry running as quickly as possible. Recruit 3 geologists and send two of them to the mountains in the north. There you'll find iron and coal.
