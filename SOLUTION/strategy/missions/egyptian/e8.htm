THE HANGMEN

VICTORY CONDITION:
Conquer the entire the map!

DEFEAT CONDITION:
You lose your western outpost (Ra5).

SITUATION:
You are on the brink of victory, but of the three warring parties, you (Ra1-Ra5) have the worst starting position. On the main island to the West you have been able to establish a bridgehead (Ra5). It is seriously being threatened by Tsu Tang (TT), who is very strong in that area. Septimus (S), who is in the South, has a more vulnerable position than Tsu Tang, but is still very resilient. Defend your outpost, obliterate Tsu Tang, and wipe Septimus from the face of the map.

LAST DIARY ENTRY OF RAMADAMSES: :

Solution from:

 Marcel Jeske

 

he entire economy is located on the main island. First, you should put a stop to gold production since there are enough gems. Production of weapons should be suspended, too. Certain tools are needed far more urgently.

Now, build a shipyard on the easternmost island to build transport and trade ships. The first transport ship can take all your soldiers to the outpost and new soldiers can be recruited on the main island. Again, tear down all towers except one. When your soldiers have reached a fighting strength of 85%, it doesn't matter if you've managed to promote your soldiers. (All of Septimus' soldiers have the highest rank from the very beginning). With a large army you can first conquer Septimus and then turn your attention to Tsu-Tang. He is not very strong and is also weakened by frequent attacks from Septimus. Septimus, on the other hand, becomes progressively stronger with time and has a lot of soldiers. Your army should be able to defeat approximately 400 enemy soldiers (all of the soldiers you have from the beginning and an additional 60 recruits should be enough if you attack early).
