THE RAGING STORM

VICTORY CONDITION:
You must completely extinguish one of your enemies!

DEFEAT CONDITION:
Your two opponents share a common border.

Your colony has been completely destroyed.

SITUATION:
Prevent the establishment of a common border between the Romans and Asians in the north. In order to ensure a lasting rule over this island, you should completely destroy one of the tribes. There is an abundance of resources and land in your area, especially in the east. A small island is worth visiting there.

STORY:

e knew the storm was coming, we could see it, stalking us like prey. The nagging fear lasted for hours. The horrible scraping of wood on rock, the ship tossed against the waves, and then fear disappeared. We knew it meant death for us all. And when death did not come, the fear grew again, the fear for a life already believed to have been lost. The storm raged another two days, but our ship remained cradled in the reef, neither sinking nor capsizing. Then yet another night came. Soaking wet, chilled to the marrow, we were menaced only by the deathly stillness following the once raging storm. No one had any idea where we were. And then - morning. The island. Like an invitation to paradise, fertile, green, with a sea full of fish.But that's what paradises are: much sought after. And we are probably the third group battling for this Garden of Eden. Although the two tribes in the north are obviously not on the best of terms, our presence will surely unite them. We must try to drive a wedge between them.

 

 

Solution from:

 David Röthlisberger

 

here is little danger that your two enemies will form a common border. Even after three hours of play, this won't happen. You should, nevertheless, hurry since your enemies, especially the Romans in the northwest, will be attacking constantly.

1.Get your grain production going early so that your manna production is in full swing later.

2.The small mountain contains enough mineral resources at the beginning, but later you'll have to exploit the resources in the mountain chain in the north. There you'll also find gold deposits in the east.

3.Once you've recruited a few soldiers, you should build two castles near the route where the Romans will launch their main attacks. Make sure that both castles are finished at the same time! Then you can repel the attacks of the Romans with fewer losses.

4.Make use of the far-off supplies of stone because, otherwise, you'll have too little in the way of building materials.

5.Concentrate on promoting as many of your soldiers as possible to their highest ranks. Thanks to the gold deposits, they should have a fighting strength of about 70%. As soon as you have some 250 soldiers, attack the Romans. Make sure that the Egyptians don't enter the fray at this point by tearing down the towers you've captured at once. With the complete destruction of the Romans the mission is finished!
