DARK RITUALS

VICTORY CONDITION:
Destroy the Romans and their settlement.

Imprison at least 10 soldiers of the southern pirate tribe in the volcano crater.

DEFEAT CONDITION:
Your colony has been completely destroyed.

Fewer than ten pirates are left.

SITUATION:
Speed is important in this mission. You have to defeat the pirates in the west quickly and then destroy the Romans. After that, you must capture the pirate groups prowling around in the southwest and get them to the inactive volcano crater with the burial grounds. At least ten pirates must be captured, before we can hope for any information from Horus regarding our whereabouts. Horus is, however, more than ready to help you get your opponents to the volcano crater. Remember not to kill all of the pirates; ten of them are needed as sacrificial offerings.

STORY:

he search for our homeland continues. We seek clues - and find hardship.

A detachment was sent out along with some building materials so a newly found island could be colonized. A couple soldiers remained behind to guard the outpost, and the rest returned to gather the workers needed. But when we returned, we found the watch guards murdered, our storage pits plundered and burned.

The island is apparently full of rival pirate factions, one of them, settling in the west, newly enriched at our expense. Retaliation is our creed. We owe at least that much to our slain comrades. Revenge will be ours, although we can't expect any support from the Romans in the southeast.

One of our spies has intercepted a message from a pirate. It spoke of a place of worship in the southwest. Located on holy ground, almost unreachable by foot, it is said to have been a burial ground for thousands of years; a place of dark rituals, priestly summonings, and sacrifices. A place offering answers but demanding human sacrifice. Maybe we'll find our answers there.

May Horus be our guide!

 

 

Solution from:

 David Röthlisberger

 

specially at the beginning, the Romans are very dangerous. Concentrate on recruiting a lot of soldiers quickly.

1. At the beginning you have nothing more than a few soldiers. The Asian settlement in the west is only weakly protected. Attack the Asians. You'll find everything you need there.

2. Expand your territory near the starting point with pioneers so that the smith is in your new settlement. Tear down all towers except for the southernmost large tower. Man this tower completely.

3. You'll find coal and gold in the mountain in the west, iron ore in the small mountain in the east.

4. Recruit soldiers early and set up your manna production. Increase your fighting strength. Position all of your soldiers at the large tower at once. This can prevent your enemy from attacking.

5. Obtain new mineral resources in order to recruit a lot of soldiers quickly. Once you have 200 soldiers at the highest rank and with a fighting strength of about 75%, begin your attack.

6. Now for the pirates… You'll find two craters in the southwest. There are a group of pirates in the one to the south. March into the crater with a priest. When the pirates storm toward your priest, have your priest use the spell "Banning Foes" to transport them to the other crater. Repeat this spell until 10 pirates have been transported there.
