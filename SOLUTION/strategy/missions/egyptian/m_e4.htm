THE EYES OF THE WORLD

VICTORY CONDITION:
Destroy all Asian rice farms and distilleries.
Erect a castle in the middle of the large swamplands.

DEFEAT CONDITION:
Your colony has been completely destroyed.
Your enemy accumulates 500 Points of Manna.

SITUATION:
A powerful Asian colony has developed in the north, and they are experts in the production of manna. It is therefore necessary to completely destroy the enemy's production by leveling all his distilleries and rice farms. In order to be able to hold this territory for the long term, a castle must be built in the center of the swampland.

Your scouts haven't found any larger supplies of building stone in the south of the island, but they overheard Asian guards speaking of a land where stones spring from the virgin soil.

STORY:

We must find the formation of two circles, called the Two Eyes of the World. We must offer sacrifices there to open the gate, which will take us home.

Unfortunately, he neglected to tell us where we could find these two eyes. And our problems at the moment are less of a religious nature and much more of a secular one. We have too little stone for building, and the Asians in the north have too many soldiers for us to threaten them. If we don't act quickly, they'll wipe us out easily and we will never behold the eyes of the world.

The Asians offer generous sacrifices to their god, Ch'ih-Yu, regularly. He is powerful, a god of war, and our chances are slim if we can't cut off their manna production soon. We have to find stone, and lots of it. We have to take possession of the swamplands in the north to defeat the Asians.

Then we can turn our attention to the Two Eyes of the World.

 

 

Solution from:

 David Röthlisberger

 

his mission is similar to the third Asian mission, but in this mission you have a bit more time. Nevertheless, you should hurry because your opponent will have no qualms about attacking you.

1. Building stone is a little scarce; there are large deposits in the south of the main island. Build a castle next to the large tower in the west. This will offer you additional protection.

2. Try to get your manna production going quickly. Promote your spearmen first!

3. 1. Exploit the mountain in the west. There you'll find gold as well as other mineral resources. With the gold you should be able to increase your fighting strength to about 70%.

4. Build about four transport ships and fill them with soldiers at the rank of sergeant. Set the soldiers ashore north of your settlement. Capture all of the towers along the swamp so that all of the enemy distilleries and rice farms are destroyed.

5. Even if you are unable to destroy all of the buildings, you'll no longer be in acute danger. Continue with similar attacks until you've reached the mission goal.
