THE PROPHECY

VICTORY CONDITION:
Destroy the Asian colonies in the southwest and east.

Station ten soldiers of the highest rank and 90% fighting strength in the Circle of Stones behind the northern colony.

 

DEFEAT CONDITION:
Your colony has been completely destroyed.

SITUATION:
Here two Asian nobles and their followers divide the land. Both are well versed in defending themselves against infantry attacks and have taken the necessary precautions. The Asian colony in the southwest has considerable supplies of gems and gold, which you should get your hands on as quickly as possible! In the northeast, behind the other Asian colony, there is a Circle of Stones, which the Asian priests consider to be of immense magical significance. Take and secure this holy area with at least ten soldiers of the highest rank and who have a fighting strength of at least 90%.

STORY:

e now have an idea of what we are looking for, and what is meant by the Eyes of the World. One of our priests had a dream the first night on this island. Since then he has had the same dream seven times: two pyramids on an island and the voice of Horus, speaking out of them:

One eye beholds ten soldiers,
The Eye of the Wolf shall be blind,
Four watery eyes as the watcher of winds,
Two smiling eyes victors shall find.

Far to the east there is a Circle of Stones. That may be the first of the eyes mentioned. We will dispatch our troops to take a closer look.

 

 

Solution from:

 David Röthlisberger

 

nly the Asians in the northeast will attack you. The ones in the south have no access to important raw materials. Coordinate your defense accordingly.

1. You won't find a whole lot of resources in the small mountain. Expand quickly to gain control of the big mountain in the southeast. There you'll also find gold./font>

2. Protect your colony by building two castles next to each other as soon as you've recruited your first soldiers. Promote your soldiers to the highest rank during the course of the game.

3. Make sure you get adequate supplies of gold and can produce manna.

4. Once you have about 200 soldiers, attack the Asians in the west. In order to destroy the castle, which is surrounded by a moat, you're going to need ballistas. Build about three or four of them.

5. Using the gold in the area previously controlled by the Asians, you can increase the fighting strength of your soldiers to about 90%.

6. Now on to the second Asian tribe. It would actually be easy to defeat the Asians in the south since they mainly have small towers. Unfortunately, many of their towers are not accessible to your soldiers because they are located on small islands. This generally is a case for the ballistas but the matter is complicated because the Asians have forests everywhere. To overcome this difficulty, you'll have to enlist the skills of your priest, unleashing a forest fire to clear the way for your ballistas. Then the Asians will be sitting ducks for your ballistas.

7. Wait until the fighting strength of your soldiers has increased to 90% and then send 10 of your soldiers to the ring of stones in the far northeast. The mission is then successfully concluded.
