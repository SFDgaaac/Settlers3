EYE FOR AN EYE

VICTORY CONDITION:
Destroy the large Roman temple in the Circle of Stones. Build a fisherman's hut beyond the small strip of desert, located in the northeast right next to the old fishing boat.

 

DEFEAT CONDITION:
Your colony has been completely destroyed.

SITUATION:
A Roman commander has settled in the southwest. His task is protecting the large temple, which is essential to his priests. The Romans are well secured, but you must destroy this temple at all costs! You must also capture the coast in the northeast of the island, which is rich in fish. This shore area is beyond a small strip of desert and can be easily identified by an ancient fishing boat lying there. There is a problem, however. To get there and be able to build a fisherman's hut you must pass through an Asian settlement.

STORY:

ne eye beholds ten soldiers -- this part of the prophecy has been fulfilled. A portal opened and brought us to another island. Here we are charged with finding the Eye of the Wolf and blinding it. A thief has reported that a peninsula in the southwest houses a Circle of Stones, with a Roman temple within it.

Apparently it is this which must be destroyed to blind the eye of the wolf. In the northwest, we have sighted huge schools of fish along the coast, near an old dilapidated fishing boat. We should make use of this bounty, even though the Asians colonizing nearby will probably not be happy about this.

 

Solution from:

 David Röthlisberger

 

n order to achieve both mission goals, you'll have to destroy almost the entire Roman and Asian settlements.

1. Exploit the resources that you find in the mountain in the southwest and the one in the north. There are gems in the mountain in the north.

2. Leave only the one large tower standing because your two opponents have you sandwiched in. Make sure you build up your colony quickly. Thanks to the gems, you can increase the fighting strength of your soldiers quickly. Don't be stingy with your offerings of beer to Horus!

3. Defend the northern mountain against the surge of expansion from the Asians by building a castle on both sides of the mountain.

4. Once you have about 200 soldiers, attack the Asians and destroy them if possible. This is not absolutely necessary, but it is better not to have an enemy at your back.

5. There is a fisherman's boat far in the northeast. Using pioneers, establish a narrow link to the coast and build a fisherman's hut there.

6. Your army's budget should be replenished in the meantime and you can now take on the Romans. The temple is located in the far southwest. Capture the towers surrounding the temple until it has been destroyed.
