THE WATCHERS OF THE WINDS

VICTORY CONDITION:
Build a guard tower on each of the islands, which is surrounded by a small circle of water in sand. These are on the periphery of the main island in all four compass directions.

Destroy one of your enemies. You have the choice.

 

DEFEAT CONDITION:
Your colony has been completely destroyed.

SITUATION:
You must destroy at least one of your enemies. This will not earn the respect of the other tribes, but the area you settle and your riches will be so great - provided you're skillful enough - that your enemies will possibly refrain from attacking.Finally, you must build the four guard towers, the four watchers of the wind.

STORY:

he Eye of the Wolf has now been rendered sightless. We spent a long time contemplating the third line of the dream prophecy. Now that the spies have returned with more information, our priests have been able to decipher the message. We understand its meaning; even if what we see is not to our liking.

We are now in the middle of an island surrounded by four hostile tribes. To keep them in line, we have to show military might and destroy one of them.

One eye beholds ten soldiers,
The Eye of the Wolf shall be blind,
Four watery eyes are the watchers of winds,
Two smiling eyes victors shall find.

We have discovered the four watery eyes. They are small ring-shaped lakes with water shallow enough to be waded through. Because there is a wind for each direction, we assumed winds were meant here and that in each wind or direction, a lake was to be found. According to the divinations of our priests, we must build a guard tower on the island in the middle of each of these lakes.

 

 

Solution from:

 David Röthlisberger

 

his mission is very similar to the eighth Asian mission, except that here all four of your opponents will attack you if you don't prevent them! Speed is everything!

1. Tear down all your towers except one large tower and position your soldiers in front of it.

2. At the beginning, use towers to expand, but tear them down immediately afterwards. You'll find gold and a rich deposit of gems in the far north of the long mountain chain. This will allow you to increase your fighting strength.

3. Get an early start on your manna production so that you can recruit at least spearmen at the rank of sergeant. In general, you should build up a very large colony so that you don't need to wait too long for supplies.

4. Once you have some 50 soldiers, primarily spearmen and swordsmen, you should attack the Asians to the south of your mountain. This will give you quick access to more mineral resources. Tear down all of the towers you capture because your enemies to the west will intervene otherwise. To avoid having to reconquer everything once again, leave the castle of the Asians in peace for the time being. Limit yourself to weakening your enemy and pushing him back.

5. When you think the Asians are weak enough and your army again numbers about 100 soldiers (ideally with some soldiers at the rank of captain), you can turn your attention to the Romans in the northwest. By this time, the Roman army will be quite large. Be prepared for some hard battles. You should be able to weaken the Romans considerably. Don't give in until the Romans are destroyed!

6. Next on your agenda is a second group of Asians in the southwest. First build up the size of your army. Now you have the worst behind you. The second group of Asians have a large army but consists mainly of privates. Your more highly promoted army should have no problems with them! As soon as your opponent has been polished off, you can look around for a place for your towers.

7. Finish off the weakened Asians!

8. And for the final, step there are the Romans in the north. They haven't been very visible. Count on the Romans having loads of soldiers, but with 200 of your own you'll have no problems dealing with them.

9. The locations for the towers, which are enclosed in a ring of water are in the middle of the path connecting your former opponents. Build a tower in each of these locations, four altogether, and you are then ready to embark on your final mission
