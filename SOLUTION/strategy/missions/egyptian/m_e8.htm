THE FALCON'S EYES

VICTORY CONDITION:
Destroy all your enemies.

Build a pyramid in each of the Circles of Stones in the northeast and southwest.

 

DEFEAT CONDITION:
Your colony has been completely destroyed.

SITUATION:
You are confronted with two highly developed opponents. Your position is weak, and that must be changed as quickly as possible. If you manage to destroy your enemies, to take possession of the two Circles of Stones, one in the northeast and the other in the southeast, and to build pyramids there. Then and only then will Horus be satisfied with you.

STORY:

he prophecy has nearly been fulfilled. After building the four watchers of the winds, we came to the island of the Two Eyes. But they haven't been brought to smile. Far from being victors, our tribe has been torn into two, and without word from one another, we have begun building colonies.

Luckily, our spies have happened on one another, and our vision has improved. We must reunite our tribes, we must find the two eyes and we must build pyramids within them so that Horus, the winged one, will carry us home.

 

Solution from:

 David Röthlisberger

 

he last mission is once again a challenging one. Your two opponents have far more raw materials than you do, and will recruit a imposing number of soldiers. You'll have to be fast!

1. There is coal on all four mountains, but iron ore is scarcer. You'll find some in the mountains in the northeast and the southwest. The mountain in the northeast also contains some deposits of gems and the one in the southwest has gold deposits. There is an abundance of stone. You should build several stone cutter's huts.

2. Connect both of your settlements as quickly as possible.

3. In order to defend yourself against both of your opponents, you should produce a lot of manna and recruit soldiers quickly. Gather all of your soldiers together in front of your only tower. Mine gold and gems at the same time.

4. Promote your soldiers. Build three castles in the middle of your two settlements, each in shooting distance of the other. Your bowmen should be promoted to the rank of captain.

5. Recruit at least 400 soldiers. Once their fighting strength is at 80%, attack both of your opponents at the same time, using 200 soldiers against the one opponent and 200 against the other opponent. By attacking both at once, you can destroy both enemy armies and aren't in danger of having your settlement destroyed by one opponent while you're attacking the other.

6. You probably won't have enough soldiers to destroy both enemies. Send newly recruited and healed soldiers to the battles on the fronts. Make use of raw materials that you've conquered from the enemy to get fresh recruits as quickly as possible.

7. In due course you should be able to defeat both your opponents.

8. Now, you only have to build the two temples in the rings of stone. Build a marketplace near each of the rings of stone. As soon as you've built both large temples, you have won! Truly an impressive performance! You can now count yourself as a true Settlers III champion!
