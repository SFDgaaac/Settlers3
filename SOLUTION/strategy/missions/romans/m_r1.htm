LOST AT SEA

VICTORY CONDITION: Take possession of the oasis in the northwest.Drive both of your opponents from the island.

DEFEAT CONDITION: Your colony has been completely destroyed

SITUATION: Unfortunately, coal resources are very limited, but as a Roman, you have an alternative source for securing coal. This new oasis is not big enough for both you and the Egyptians. Jupiter will not listen to the prayers of your priests, except they be the only voices he hears. It will be necessary to completely destroy your newfound enemy.

STORY:

have traveled Mare Nostrum and have visited the lands of the dark-skinned giants, where the sun lies in the north at noon. I have visited the Islands of Bliss, and the Pillars of Hercules have seen me more often than my wife. But never have I encountered a storm such as this. In all my days and travels I thought I knew every island in Mare Nostrum, but alas I have no idea where we are. I don't even recognize the stars here. But curse Neptune I will not, for he has led us to fertile soil and a beautiful land.

Beauty is often coveted, and we have heard that an Egyptian settlement in the north has laid claim to this land as well...Our spies have reports of a holy oasis far to the northwest. Perhaps we will find answers there.

 

 

Solution from:

 David Röthlisberger

 

he first mission is already quite a tough one. Charcoal maker's huts (you should have at least ten) are as crucial to your success here as is the smooth building up of your economy. It is also important to get your manna production going as soon as possible.

1. Begin this mission just like most of the other Settlers missions: provide building materials by building a stonecutter's hut, two woodcutter's huts and a sawmill. Expand your territory by building small towers. Caution: In this mission (as in most of the other ones) you have to deal with a very aggressive opponent. Do NOT expand your territory too fast by building plenty of small towers, for that is only going to provoke your opponent, who's military strength is far superior to your own at the beginning of the game. It is better to leave only one single tower standing and to crush all other towers as soon as they have been occupied. A single tower is always easier to defend and will always attract less attention than several towers. Still, you can build some watchtowers later on to keep an eye on your territory.

2. Build a medium residence and scout the western mountains. You are bound to find plenty of gold and iron, but there is hardly any coal and it can only be found in two places. However, you should still build some coalmines there.

3. Continue building up your economy just as usual. Once your food production and your industry are in full swing you should focus on the production of manna.

4. Possibly, you have already been attacked by your opponents at this point. However, you should have been able to repel those attacks with the soldiers you started with and the ones you have recently produced. In any case, you should build a hospital: it will be a great help.

5. Now the coalmines will no longer produce much, so you will have to start building charcoal maker's huts. To keep a charcoal maker's hut going it takes approx. one sawmill, two woodcutter's huts and one forester's hut. There is plenty of space for those buildings in the west and east.

6. By now your manna production should be flourishing, so you should be able to produce soldiers at the rank of sergeant. Keep on building charcoal maker's huts until you have about ten of them. Once you have soldiers at the rank of captain, you should build a castle in the east next to the border of the superior yellow opponent. Later on you should build a second castle. Fill those two castles with soldiers and they should be able to withstand any enemy attack. Depending on the behavior of your blue opponent you should consider building one or two castles next to his border.

7. Now that you are safe from your opponents' attacks, you can concentrate on building up your army. Mine gold until your troops' fighting strength has reached approx. 70%.

8. 200 soldiers at the rank of captain should be enough to attack and vanquish your yellow opponent. Depending on your troops' condition, you can move straight on to the blue opponent and vanquish him, too. All you have to do now to win the mission is to conquer the northern oasis by building a tower at its edge.
