THE ORACLE

VICTORY CONDITION: Drive out the opposing Roman tribes in the north.Amass 100 iron bars in storage.

DEFEAT CONDITION: Your colony has been completely destroyed.

SITUATION: You have to drive the opposing Roman tribes away. Because you only have very limited space at the beginning, you should produce manna quickly so that you can transform desert into fertile land.

STORY:

he priests have showed us our way north to another island. It is equally unfamiliar to us and not nearly as fertile. Vast deserts stretch through the north, and our priests will have to work great wonders to get our crops to grow here.

Still farther north, a fellow Roman colony has been spotted, but the oracle forbids us from making peace with them. We are supposed to conquer this land, and it is also time for us to pay homage to Vulcanus. We must gather 100 iron bars for the tribute.

If I only knew what was going on here. We are like puppets to the gods, and know not what they have in store for us.

 

Solution from:

 David Röthlisberger

 

our most important units are now the pioneers: with their help you should claim the northern mountains, where you will find plenty of much needed resources such as iron, coal and gold. Instead of using donkeys to transport food to the mines and bring the resources back to your settlement, you should create a narrow corridor with your pioneers to connect the mountains and your settlement. Here a couple of things to keep in mind:

1. At first, expand your territory by building towers, but remember to always crush any tower you've just manned with a soldier. A single tower will be enough for now.

2. Expand your settlement rapidly and build residences to create more Settlers. Turn some of those Settlers into pioneers and let them claim the area mentioned above from your settlement to the northern mountains.

3. Thanks to the various goods lying around on this map, you can begin producing soldiers even before you have built your first mine. Those soldiers are quite handy when it comes to defending your settlement against any possible attack.

4. Once again, remember to produce manna at an early stage in the game! If you do not have much room to build the necessary buildings, use magical spells to turn the desert into arable soil.

5. Thanks to the northern mountains, which you should have claimed by now, you now have plenty of resources to start recruiting more soldiers. In the meantime, you should have accumulated some manna to begin promoting your soldiers. Speed up your production of goods by building two iron smelting works and two weapon smith's works. Of course, to keep your production running smoothly, you will also have to build some more coalmines. You can speed up the promotion of your soldiers by building four small temples and five to six wineries.

6. Keep mining gold until your troops have an fighting strength of approx. 80%.

7. With an army of approx. 200 fully promoted soldiers you should be able to defeat both of your opponents in a single battle.

8. All you have to do now is produce 100 iron bars!
