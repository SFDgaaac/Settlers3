THE SALUTE

VICTORY CONDITION: Clear the Asians off the island.

Your ally must get enough iron so that they can recruit 100 soldiers.

DEFEAT CONDITION: You or your ally have been completely destroyed.

SITUATION: A small Roman colony is seeking your help and has settled to the north of the large stone field. There are no natural sources of iron available to them, and they will certainly perish without help. Even wood and stone are in scarce supply there. Make sure you provide them sufficient support and drive out the Asians before you or your ally are annihilated.

STORY:

e dared to take the sea again and the favorable winds of Aolus drove us to yet another unfamiliar coast. Soon after landing, a cry for help from a small Roman settlement reached us. They have been under attack from a menacing Asian tribe for weeks. We had no choice but to help them. Our priests believe they will share information about our homeland if we succeed in helping them.

 

Solution from:

 David Röthlisberger

 

ou can ignore your ally for the moment: he is more than able to defend himself. You should only intervene if your opponent attacks your ally's castle. Tip: your ally's hospital can heal your troops, too!

1. In this mission it is once again a good idea to leave just a single tower standing. By doing so, you will attract less attention from your opponent. This is important for building up your economy in time before your opponent starts attacking you. You will find plenty of gold, iron and coal on the mountain in your territory.

2. You should start producing huge amounts of manna as soon as you can!

3. Wait until you have defeated your opponent before you send resources to your ally!

4. In the meantime, your ally will have lost some of his towers. You don't have to worry about that right now, but do NOT try to recapture those towers immediately! In most cases, your opponent will be satisfied with capturing a few of your ally's towers. However, at some point he will attack you, too. By then you should have produced enough soldiers to fend off his occasional attacks.

5. Increase your fighting strength to approx. 80-90%. Once you have approx. 250 soldiers (level 3) you should make your move and attack your opponent!

6. As soon as you have defeated your Asian opponent, start delivering iron bars, some coal and some weapons to your ally. He is going to expand his territory rapidly now and will start recruiting soldiers soon. Once he has 100 soldiers, you have won.
