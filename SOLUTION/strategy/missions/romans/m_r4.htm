THE HOLY MOUNTAIN

VICTORY CONDITION: Drive out the Egyptians in the west.

DEFEAT CONDITION: Your colony has been completely destroyed.

SITUATION: In order to get to the mountain you will need to cross water. The land connection takes far too long and is too dangerous. There are also those two hostile tribes of Egyptians to consider.

The tribe which resides far in the west must be driven out.

STORY:

ollowing the instructions of our priests, we set sail for the northern territories with unknown stars above us.We came to a strange island that is ring-shaped in the form of an atoll. In the middle of the ring, there is a mountain and this mountain means trouble.

An Egyptian tribe that lives nearby regards this mountain as sacred Holy Land. We will have to convince them otherwise, for this mountain is our main supply of resources.

 

Solution from:

 David Röthlisberger

 

or a change, this is mission is a less hectic one, for your opponent will leave you alone!

1.You will hardly find anything useful on your island: some iron in the northern hill and some coal in the eastern mountain. That's it. Make sure to claim the northern peninsula along with the mountain! Before building up your heavy industry, you should build a trade ship and a transport ship first. Claim the entire northern mountain with pioneers, and then build two or three coalmines, an iron mine and a gold mine.

2. Now that you have enough resources, you can take your time building up your settlement, producing manna, piling up gold and recruiting soldiers.

3. You don't have to attack the Egyptians in the north. However, you MUST get rid of the Egyptians in the west. 200 soldiers should be enough for this task.
