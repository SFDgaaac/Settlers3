THE ANCIENT MAP

VICTORY CONDITION: Take possession of the Circle of Stones in the north.

DEFEAT CONDITION: Your colony has been completely destroyed.

SITUATION: Your goal is to take possession of the Circle of Stones in the north. You have a little time to do this, since your immediate neighbors show little interest in expanding their territory. They are, however, guarding the narrow passage to the northern half of the island.

STORY:

e found a seafarer's map in an old mining tunnel buried deep within the mountain. Only one other island is depicted on it, and it is in the shape of an hourglass. Since the Ring Island had no other information to impart, we have left it behind.

There is said to be a holy place there, far to the north of the new island: yet another Circle of Stones. It is a gate to another world, and perhaps our passage home.

But before we can attend to those matters, a couple of Egyptians need our attention, since they stand directly in our way.

 

Solution from:

 David Röthlisberger

 

his is by far the most difficult mission you've had to face so far. Your opponent starts attacking you early with plenty of soldiers, and once again you hardly have any coal to speak of. Yet again, the "Keep only one tower” strategy is the key to victory. Luckily, you only have to deal with a single opponent on this map, for the yellow opponent is not going to build up a colony of his own. He only has six large towers and a couple of soldiers.

1.Keep building small towers in the east and west until those two small mountains are in your hands. Remember to crush all towers once you've manned them with a soldier, and leave only your first tower standing. Once the mountains are yours, expand your territory with pioneers only.

2.North of the two mountains you will find some coal, and there is also some iron to be found in the south. Keep on mining coal until there is nothing left.

3.Build up your economy quickly, but don't forget about the manna! You must produce soldiers as soon as you can: the higher their offensive strength, the better for you!

4.Gather all your soldiers around your only tower. This will prevent your opponent from attacking. You have been lucky, if he hasn't attacked you by now. However, you should still build a healer's quarters.

5.Now that there no more coal to mine, it is time to start building charcoal maker's huts. By now your pioneers should have claimed a lot of land in the east. Despite the fact that most of this land is thickly covered with trees, you should use foresters to ensure that your charcoal makers (you should have approx. six) have always enough wood.

6.There is some gold to be found in the western mountain. It's not much, but every little bit helps. You can use the 'Midas Touch' to gain an fighting strength of approx. 70%. Start recruiting a huge army consisting of fully promoted soldiers, conquer the six towers held by your yellow opponent, and leave a full complement of soldiers in each tower. Thanks to the gold and coal you now have looted, your soldiers will feel great! However, the other Egyptians will not respond kindly to this latest "acquisition” of yours and will strike soon. Nevertheless, your soldiers should now be able to deal with any attack.

7.Recruit some more soldiers and heal your wounded. Now you should be able to take the circle of stones in the north
