THE WINE WAR

VICTORY CONDITION: A small temple must be built within the Circle of Stones.

Amass 200 manna points.

DEFEAT CONDITION: Your colony has been completely destroyed.

SITUATION: In the north, on the other side of a long land bridge, there is a Circle of Stones. Your task is to take possession of this and build a small temple within it.

STORY:

e stepped through the portal into a new world , and suddenly we could get a glimpse of a plan that shrouded our journey. Our new lands are called the Winelands, for we dicovered a great wine, a variety we are not familiar with. Jupiter has instructed our priests to offer generous amounts of this wine in the Circle of Stones in the north.

Well, big wars have broken out with considerably less cause.

 

 

Solution from:

 David Röthlisberger

 

ou should not have any problems finding enough resources in this mission. However, the problem is to actually FIND them! Huge mountains surround them; the only gap is in the north. Exactly south of your starting position you may find some iron. A little bit to the west from there you may find some gold, and some coal in the east. Another source of coal lies to the west of your starting position. Again you have to deal with a very aggressive opponent, so it's a good idea to crush all newly built towers once a soldier has manned them. Build a tower as close to the mountain as possible and claim the rest with pioneers.

1.Once you have found the resources start building up your economy. As soon as you have enough soldiers you should build a castle in the north and man it with a full complement of soldiers. Build a second castle right next to the first one to protect your border effectively against all possible attacks.

2.Remember to produce manna. Admittedly, it's quite hard to produce any on this terrain… The only place to grow wine is along the river in the north.

3.Since there is plenty of gold in the mountains it should be easy for you to upgrade your soldiers' fighting strength to approx. 80%.

4.Your opponent is not a pushover. To beat him you must have quite a large army of soldiers at the rank of captain.

5.Once you have built the temple in the circle of stones, all you have to do is produce 200 manna points, that is, if you haven't produced that many already. Every line under the miracles in the Settlers menu symbolizes 10 manna points.
