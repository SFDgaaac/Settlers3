WET FEET

VICTORY CONDITION: 150 bars of gold must be produced.

Take possession of the wine storage area in the Egyptian hinterland.

DEFEAT CONDITION: Your colony has been completely destroyed.

SITUATION: You must find the footpath leading to the mountain in the northwest and secure it to ensure a reserve supply of resources. If you succeed, it should not be difficult to defeat the Egyptians and bring the wine back home.

STORY:

upiter has not yet gotten enough wine, or at least he is not willing to tolerate our enemy having large amounts of the precious drink stored in his territory. We must seize these supplies for Jupiter and at the same time smelt 150 bars of gold for him.

Yet there is no gold to be found here. In the northwest there is said to be gold deposits in a large mountain. But, first of all, we have to get there. Our enemy is blocking the way.

One of our spies, though, maintains that there is a footpath through the muck...

 

Solution from:

 David Röthlisberger

 

ou may choose to take the hard way across the mud flats to reach the resources in the northern mountains. However, there is an easier way of getting resources: just send your geologists to the eastern mountains!

1.Build up your colony and promote your soldiers early.

2.Those eastern mountains will provide you with enough resources for a while. In addition, there are coal and iron lying around all over the map. Claim the territory around those resources with your pioneers.

3.Luckily, your opponent will launch his first attack against you quite late in the game. If you begin producing manna early you should be able to produce soldiers at the rank of sergeant soon. Send a small squad of soldiers to your opponent to take away his mountain. Remember to SAVE your game first!

4.You should be able to capture and hold the towers around the mountain. Use pioneers to create a narrow path between the mountain and your colony. Now you can really start recruiting soldiers.

5.In any case, build a castle next to the mountain and man it with a full complement of soldiers. You should also move the rest of your soldiers to the mountains; you wouldn't want to lose it, right?

6.The loss of his mountain should've substantially weakened your opponent; he shouldn't be much of a problem now.

7.Just produce 150 gold bars, and you've won this mission!
