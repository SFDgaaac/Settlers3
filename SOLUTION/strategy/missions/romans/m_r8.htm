ASCENSION

VICTORY CONDITION: Take possession of the sea enclosed by the mountains.

DEFEAT CONDITION: Your colony has been completely destroyed.

SITUATION: Although your goal lies in the west, you must first deal with stabilizing your own situation, since you can expect strong enemy contact on the main island in the west. In spite of this, you must be strong enough at the end to overrun the western island and to find the crystal sea.

STORY:

upiter is satisfied, and has led us to an island that, hidden behind high mountains and protected by zealous natives, is the gate to our homeland - a sea as clear as a mountain stream.

At present, though, we have numerous other problems: very scarce supplies of raw materials and too little space to expand. We have to explore the surrounding islands. In the north there is said to be a lot of land and an abundance of resources.The way home, however, lies to the west.

 

Solution from:

 David Röthlisberger

 

his mission is as laid back as the fourth one of this campaign. To get some resources you should conquer at least one of the two islands in the northeast. Remember to build another castle on your outpost to make sure that you won't lose your foothold on that island (and the mission!).

1. Take your time. Since there are not many resources to be found on your islands you should build a trade ship and a transport ship before building a tool or a weapon smith's works. Otherwise, you won't have enough iron bars for building ships.

2. Take the neighboring island with approx. 20 pioneers. The mountain on that island is bursting with resources: gold, iron and coal. Get those goodies to your main island and deliver food to your outpost in return.

3. Keep on producing gold until your fighting strength reaches approx. 90%.

4. You haven't forgotten to produce manna and weapons, right? Transport those weapons to your outpost, which still exists, thanks to the second mountain. Build a barracks and two large residences to recruit approx. 200 soldiers. This should be enough, for only one of your opponents has built a proper colony. No problems here…

5. As soon as you have defeated your blue opponent you can start capturing the castles around the lake and finish this campaign.
