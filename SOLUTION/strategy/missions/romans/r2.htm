VICTORY CONDITION: Conquer the entire map!

DEFEAT CONDITION: You lose your northwestern outpost (S2).

SITUATION: A civil war has decimated a large part of Your colony (S1). The rebels (Re) have withdrawn across the North sea, and are colonizing there. I've heard reports that the rebels have allied themselves with a group of pirates in the West. (P). One of your scout ships has been stranded in the Northwest, not too far from your colony (S2). You'll need to establish an outpost there and attack the enemy from this position.

Solution from:

 jsd98

 

ere you can learn the basics of maritime trade! You may easily be playing this mission for hours before you find out how to build ships and transport goods with them... Your Settlers are spread out on two islands: the main island (nobody can attack you on that island, which is why one tower is enough) and the island which is going to become the main battlefield.

At the beginning you should build a residence on each of your islands (preferably a medium-sized one). Expand your territory with pioneers only. On your main island you should get your timber industry going as quickly as you can. Build a shipyard, then a transport ship and finally a trade ship. Of course, you should get the production of food and the mining of resources going too, including smelting works, smith’s huts, barracks etc..

Later on you can also build a vineyard and a temple.

Save resources by not expanding your territory on the outpost island by building towers. Instead, turn your new arrived Settlers into pioneers and keep on expanding: first to the west until you reach the border, then to the south.

Start sending over soldiers from the main island as soon as you have established a ship route between the two islands. Start building a trade ship now. In the east and south of your outpost try to reach the small land bridge, build a tower there and man it with a full complement of soldiers. That should keep an easy opponent like the one on this map at bay!

Now start having your woodcutters chop down the woods on the outpost island. You should also start mining resources and producing food (build mines, farms etc.). Now start producing plenty of soldiers on your outpost, and, soon, you will be ready to strike

 

Solution from:

 Tobias

 

ou start with two islands. However, the game is over once you lose your outpost on the western island. You should try to build a transport ship as quickly as you can (shipyard!) to get all of your soldiers from the main island to the outpost.

Don’t worry about your main island being attacked: the computer-controlled opponents in “The Settlers III” never build ships! The first opponents to attack you are the Roman pirates in the south. However, just like the opponent in the north, those pirates are not very strong, which makes an early attack on them quite easy. Do not wait too long before attacking your opponent in the north either, because the more time he has, the stronger he gets. Still, for a very long time he will be quite easy to beat. Remember to transport enough soldiers from the main island to your ‘outpost’; two or three transport ships are recommended. Don’t forget to build residences on your main island on a regular basis. Otherwise, all your carriers will be turned into soldiers and your economy will collapse rapidly!

By the way: you should try your hand at transporting goods across the sea in this mission.

 

Solution from:

 Marcel Jeske

 

n your main island you can conquer new territory with both pioneers and towers. Woodcutters will then make room for buildings. There should not be any problem finding enough stone. An iron smelting works and a shipyard will make sure that you will be able to build enough transport ships for transporting all of your soldiers to your outpost. You don’t have to have more than a single tower on your main island. There is plenty of space for farms in the south, so providing your miners with food shouldn’t be a problem. Fishing, however, is hardly worth the effort, and you don’t need pigs if there is enough bread.

Expand your outpost and look for gold in the north to boost your soldiers’ strength. Two fishermen at the west coast should be sufficient for a gold and coal mine each. Once you feel strong enough (60% or more) you can strike against the pirates (yellow). They shouldn’t be much of problem. After you have recruited some more soldiers you can deal with the blue opponent…
