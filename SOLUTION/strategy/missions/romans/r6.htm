VOLCANO ISLAND

VICTORY CONDITION: Conquer the northern archipelago (Ra)!

DEFEAT CONDITION:The Egyptians (Ra) amass more than 100 gold bullion.

SITUATION: Your two colonies (S1, S2) separated by the sea are still very weak from the war. The Egyptian colonies have been devastated by a recent volcanic eruption. You have to stop Ramadamses (Ra) from transforming his archipelago into a fortress.

6. DIARY ENTRY OF SEPTIMUS MARIUS:

e have been visited by fate...or divine wrath. We conquered the island group, and had even managed to drive Tsu Tang out of his two more northern islands when a volcano erupted in our midst, devastating all of the islands in the north. Only a small colony in the northwest remains.

Ramadamses took advantage of the disaster to advance from the northeast and occupy the island group. He then began assisting Tsu Tang's beleaguered island and so forged an alliance.

But Ramadamses may be slinging the noose around his own neck. If he curries favor with Tsu Tang in the belief that he can use this dangerous warrior for his own purposes, he is in for a surprise. Even if he manages to defeat us using Tsu Tang as the sword, he will himself be impaled on the blade. Ramadamses should have remained a sculptor....

Though the raw materials from the pirate island are dwindling to an end, at least Flavius was able to save the fleet. We must repel Ramadamses before we can advance further. I can only hope that Jupiter has the strength to protect me from the gods of my foes....

 

Solution from:

 Marcel Jeske

 

his time you have to deal with Ramadamses only. Because of the limit (100 gold bars) this mission appears to be very hard at first glance. However, it is quite easy to win. Forget about the neighboring islands, and crush all of military building on the main island. Your infrastructure is almost finished right at the beginning, so you can ignore most of the warning messages you receive. Produce gold and store it in the storage areas at once. Transport all of your soldiers to your main island. Once your fighting strength has reached 65%, you can wipe the weak Ramadamses from the face of the earth.

On one occasion my opponent did not have any gold and about 80 soldiers. I attacked him with about 130 soldiers (70% offensive strength) and lost only ONE spearman!

 

Solution from:

n this map you have to defeat your opponent in the north. Your economy is already up and running right from the start. Build a shipyard as soon as you can and transport your soldiers to the land bridge near the blue opponent's territory. Since your opponent is still busy building up his economy, you've got a great chance of overrunning him!
