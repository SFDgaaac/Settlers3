THE TRAITOR'S WRATH

VICTORY CONDITION:  Conquer the Egyptian settlement in the middle of the map (Ra2), and succeed in freeing the approaches to the inland sea (M) !

DEFEAT CONDITION: You lose all your buildings.

SITUATION: Your first task is to put Ramadamses (Ra1, Ra2) out of action. It will probably be enough for you to exploit his overstretched borders and take over the colony on the peninsula in the middle of the map (Ra2). Tsu Tang (TT) will then take care of the rest.

7. DIARY ENTRY OF SEPTIMUS MARIUS:

amadamses already has one foot in the grave - he just doesn't know it yet. The fool toddles behind Tsu Tang because he thinks he will be rewarded out of gratitude, but if Tsu Tang defeats me, he will swat Ramadamses away like a bothersome fly.

 

Solution from:

 Marcel Jeske

 

our opponent is not strong enough to withstand an early attack. Press P for pause right at the beginning and crush all towers except for the large one. Continue playing and gather all of your swordsmen near your opponent's (yellow) border. Once all of your soldiers have arrived, you can attack the first castle. It is crucial now to keep your soldiers together, otherwise they won't survive the battle. Once you have conquered the first castle and survived the ensuing battle, it is easy to finish off your opponent's remaining towers and his last castle.
