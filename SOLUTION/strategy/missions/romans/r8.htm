Basically, there is hardly any building that still needs to be built. However, you should build a storage area for storing gold only. You could also need some more Settlers (residences). Once the production of gold is in full swing, you may start attacking your opponents. First, you should divide the land by moving westward. Then you should start conquering the southern part. The following strategy reduces your potential losses to a minimum, and gives you a chance of winning the final battle without having to recruit a lot of new soldiers.

Attack military buildings, conquer them, and then destroy them. Your opponent's forces must be prevented from reaching the site of a crushed building. To do this, surround any attacking soldiers with all your swordsmen. The computer-controlled opponents will try to reach a (destroyed) tower before they start defending themselves. Therefore, it is quite easy to stop them while they are still trying to reach their destination. Keeping this in mind it is quite easy to finish off all towers one by one, thereby winning the Roman campaign. (This strategy works almost every time.)
